WHAT IS CROWDFUNDING?

Crowdfunding is the process by which individuals and organizations raise money from a large number of people. There are many crowdfunding web applications out there. GoFundMe with tens of millions of users is one of the most popular online crowdfunding platforms. However, GoFundMe, as well as most other crowdfunding web applications, are for-profit organizations and platforms. Erdata Crowdfunding aims to provide an online fundraising platform on par with these other web applications while remaining a nonprofit so that users can make the most of their money, both in terms of giving and receiving.

HOW DOES ERDATA WORK?

Registered Users are users navigating the website while being logged in. These users have access to all parts of the web application. The system will allow these users to view information on all the pages of the website: “Home”, “View Fundraisers”, “Learn More”. Most importantly, it will allow Registered Users to post and start fundraisers and/or make contributions (donate) to fundraisers. It will track and make available to them the progress of their fundraisers, as well as their transactions (payment/contribution history).

Guest Users are users navigating the website without being logged in. The system will allow these users to browse the website and view information on the “Home” and “Learn More” pages, as well as view all the fundraisers that have been posted along with their progress under the “View Fundraisers” page. The system will not allow Guest Users to start/post fundraisers nor donate to fundraisers. It will give Guest Users the option to register and/or login to become Registered Users in order to access those functionalities.

TIPS AND IDEAS

  • Begin with a realistic goal
  • Update your fundraiser regularly
  • Adjust your goal as needed
  • Follow up on your fundraiser

FAQ

Anyone can set up a fundraiser.
Individuals, charities, organizations, political campaigns, etc. can all make use of Erdata.
People can use Erdata for themselves, friends and family, or even complete strangers in random acts of kindness. You can raise money for medical expenses, education costs, volunteer programs, youth sports, funerals & memorials, etc.
Your fundraiser will remain live until you choose to turn off donations or remove the campaign altogether. You can even leave your campaigns active although your goal has been met.
